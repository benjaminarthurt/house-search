Portrait photographs of members of the Skinner family, collected from public sources including Find a Grave. See also Skinner Family Network for full genealogical context.

Timothy Warner Skinner (1827–1915)

Attorney, Oswego County Surrogate, village president, and builder of the house.

Dr. Avery Warner Skinner (1870–1937)

Son of Timothy W. Skinner; born in the house. Served in the NY State Education Department.

Elizabeth Viola "Lizzie" Skinner Stone (1857–1943)
