## Research significance

This memorial document — almost certainly published in a Universalist church publication around the time of Charles Rufus Skinner's death on July 5, 1928 — provides the most complete single-document snapshot of the extended Skinner family available online. It explicitly names T. W. Skinner as Charles R. Skinner's brother and confirms he was already deceased by 1928.

Key findings for this research project:

1. **T. W. Skinner confirmed as brother of Charles R. Skinner**: "A brother was Hon. T. W. Skinner of this Village." (Past tense "was" = T. W. Skinner predeceased Charles R. Skinner, who died July 5, 1928.)

2. **Siblings of Charles R. Skinner** (= also siblings of T. W. Skinner, all children of Avery Skinner):
   - T. W. Skinner (brother, predeceased 1928, Mexico village)
   - Mrs. Maurice L. Wright (sister, also predeceased 1928, of Oswego) — mother of Avery Skinner Wright

3. **Total children**: Charles R. Skinner was one of **six children** of Avery Skinner.

4. **Family name for T. W. Skinner**: Confirmed styled "Hon." (Honorable), consistent with his service as Surrogate of Oswego County (1863–1883) and as village president.

## Genealogical implications

**T. W. Skinner's death date:** Unknown. He was alive April 24, 1907 (80th birthday, Mexico Independent article). He was dead by July 1928 (this memorial). The Mexico Independent archive gap (1908 onward not yet digitized in NYS Historic Newspapers) prevents locating his obituary. The Oswego Daily Palladium archive is also not indexed for this period. The exact death date remains to be found via Oswego County Historian, physical newspaper archives, or a future digitization.

**T. W. Skinner's parent clarification:** Per the Charles R. Skinner memorial, the mother was **Charlotte Prior (Stebbins) Skinner** (born 1802, died 1888 per the Wikisource Biographical Dictionary entry for Charles R. Skinner). The "Who's Who" entry for Avery Warner Skinner (1937) gives his parents as "Timothy (V.) and Sarah L. (Rose) Skinner" — confirming that Sarah L. Rose was T. W. Skinner's second wife (not mother). First wife was Sarah Elizabeth Calkins (1833–1861). T. W. Skinner's mother was Charlotte Prior (Stebbins) Skinner.
