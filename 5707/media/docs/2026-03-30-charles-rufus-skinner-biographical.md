# Charles Rufus Skinner: Biographical Profile and Family Connection to Mexico, NY

**Birth/Death:** August 4, 1844 – June 30, 1928
**Birthplace:** Union Square, Town of Mexico, Oswego County, New York
**Death Location:** Pelham Manor, New York (pneumonia; ashes interred at Brookside Cemetery, Watertown, NY)

## Career Overview

### Newspaper Industry
- **Editor, Watertown Daily Times:** 1870–1874
- **Owner/Partner:** Purchased third interest in Watertown Daily Times and Reformer (associates: Lotus Ingalls, Beman Brockway)
- **Editor, Watertown Daily Republican:** 1885–1886
- **City Editor, Watertown Daily Times:** 1886

### Political Career
- **New York State Assembly Member** (Jefferson Co., 1st District): 1877, 1878, 1879, 1880, 1881
- **U.S. Representative:** Elected to 47th U.S. Congress to fill vacancy caused by Warner Miller's election to U.S. Senate; re-elected for subsequent terms
- **State Superintendent of Public Instruction:** 1895–1904
- **National Education Association President:** 1897

## Significance for Skinner Family Research
