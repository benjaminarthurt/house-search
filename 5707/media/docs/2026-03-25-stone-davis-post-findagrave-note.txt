Relevant excerpt:

"The funeral of Private Lyman C. Stacy, a member of Company A, 7th Infantry, A.E.F., whose death occurred in France, July 27, 1918, from wounds received at the second battle of the Marne was held from Whitney & Hill's undertaking rooms in Mexico Sunday, May 7, at 12:30 p.m. and from the M.E. church at 1 o'clock. The Rev. James Mathews officiated and the committal services at South Richland cemetery was in charge of Stone-Davis Post No. 384, American Legion."

Source attribution on memorial page: Sandy Creek News, May 11, 1922.
