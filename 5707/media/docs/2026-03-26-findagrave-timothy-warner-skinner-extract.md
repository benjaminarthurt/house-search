# Find a Grave: Timothy Warner Skinner (1827–1915)
Source: https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/109782554/timothy-warner-skinner

Obituary-style excerpt (abridged for context):
- Born Apr 24, 1827; educated at Mexico Academy; farmer/teacher; admitted to bar 1857; law partnerships to 1870; Republican; Oswego County Surrogate (elected 1863; re-elected 1870, 1876).
- Village president; trustee of Mexico Academy; trustee of First Methodist Church; prominent Mason.
- Survived by brothers James and Charles R. (former NY superintendent of public instruction); son Avery W. (Albany Education Dept.); daughter Mrs. J. B. Stone of Auburn.
- Marriages: (Sarah) Elizabeth Calkins (1856; d. 1861); Sarah L. Rose (m. 5 Aug 1862).
- Daily Times (Oswego), 30 Mar 1915.
