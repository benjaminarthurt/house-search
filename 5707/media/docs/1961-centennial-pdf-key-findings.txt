Key findings from Mexico Independent Centennial Issue 1861-1961_text.pdf

Important recovered lines relevant to the house/address research:

1. Street-name bridge:
- "Center Avenue (Scenic)" appears in the text.
- This is a strong direct textual bridge showing that the route later known as Scenic was at one point referred to as Center Avenue in the centennial issue's historical discussion.

2. Skinner / Legion continuity:
- The centennial issue states that in 1944 the American Legion purchased "the former home of A. W. Skinner on Scenic Avenue."
- This strongly links the Skinner family line to the later Legion property.

3. Railroad / Scenic / Liberty geometry:
- Another caption references "Looking at the intersection of Main and Scenic (about 1915)" and nearby discussion references Cemetery (Liberty), which helps align the modern street geometry.
