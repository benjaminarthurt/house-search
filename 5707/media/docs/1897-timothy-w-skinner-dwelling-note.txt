Mexico Independent, 11 August 1897

Recovered notice references Timothy W. Skinner and states he has a "dwelling in the village of Mexico, N.Y."

Transcribed substance:
- notice to creditors / assignee matter
- references "Timothy W. Skinner, lately doing business in the village of Mexico, N.Y."
- directs claims to assignee
- specifically says "at his dwelling in the village of Mexico, N.Y."

Why this matters:
- supports the nomination narrative that Timothy W. Skinner was a prominent Mexico village lawyer with a significant residence there
- does not by itself give the exact street address
- does strengthen the case that the house associated with Skinner was a real village residence in active use by the 1890s
