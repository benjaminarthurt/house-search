# Skinner Family Genealogy: Mexico, Oswego County, New York

## Documented Family Members

### Timothy W. Skinner
- **Position:** Village President of Mexico, NY (1890)
- **Business:** Banking industry connections; succeeded O. H. Whitney and Cyrus Whitney in banking business
- **Significance:** Leadership role in village governance during late 19th century

### Avery Skinner
- **Birth:** June 9, 1796, Westmoreland, New Hampshire
- **Death:** November 24, 1876, Union Square, Oswego County, New York
- **Political Positions:**
  - New York State Assembly (1831-1832)
  - County Treasurer (1826-1838)
  - County Judge (1828-1839)
  - New York State Senate, representing Oswego County (1838-1842)
- **Community Service:** Secretary of the Board of Trustees for Rensselaer Oswego Academy (Mexico Military Academy)
- **Academy Connection:** Rensselaer Oswego Academy incorporated April 13, 1826

### Charles Rufus Skinner
- **Birth:** August 4, 1844, Union Square, Town of Mexico, Oswego County, New York
- **Death:** June 30, 1928 (pneumonia)
- **Political Career:**
  - New York State Assembly (Jefferson Co., 1877-1881)
  - U.S. Representative, 47th Congress (filled vacancy from Warner Miller election to Senate)
  - New York State Superintendent of Public Instruction (1895-1904)
  - President, National Education Association (1897)
- **Newspaper Career:**
  - Editor, Watertown Daily Times (1870-1874)
  - Owner/Partner, Watertown Daily Times and Reformer
  - Editor, Watertown Daily Republican (1885-1886)
  - City Editor, Watertown Daily Times (1886)
