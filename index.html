The Timothy Skinner House

A large brick Italianate residence built c. 1850 at 5707 Scenic Ave (formerly Railroad Street), Mexico, Oswego County, New York — listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1991.

NRHP Listed 1991 Built c. 1850 Italianate brick Skinner family Mexico, NY

House History

Built by Timothy Warner Skinner, lawyer and Oswego County Surrogate. Home to the Skinner family for generations before passing to the American Legion.

Skinner Family

Timothy W. Skinner (1827–1915), his son Dr. Avery Warner Skinner, and their extended family are documented with portraits, genealogy, and historical newspaper records.

Archival Media

Historic maps, newspaper clippings, NRHP nomination documents, FindAGrave portraits, site-visit photographs, and preserved web sources — all indexed and browsable.

NRHP Nomination

The house was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1991 (ref 91000526) as part of the Mexico Village Multiple Property Submission.
